About Me

     Hi, I'm Prashant, a Software Developer who loves building products. On a practical front, I am focused, directed, dedicated and an enthusiastic person. Being an active member and as part of the college coding club incorporated the spirit of teamwork and getting along well with all people in the team. The quality of team player is desirable for a success of any company or research group. As a hobby, I write python scripts to automate daily activities and build personalized websites for others.


    I wish to work and gain exposure in any company or research stream that offers a chance to apply all the practical knowledge gained over the years. In the future, I aim to see myself working in a company or research area which offers me an opportunity to resolve and provide solutions to real time problems. The field should be open to ideas to allow development of new ways to tackle the problems.


    As an Engineer, I want to help people overcome fuzzy human protocols and leverage technology to make the world a better place. I posit that process kills efficiency and simple is always better. I code, write, read, speak, learn and listen. Most of all, I am as passionate about my craft today as I was when I wrote my first line of code.


    As a leader, I believe that every person has the power to change the world if they have a positive attitude, an open, optimistic mind, and a desire to thrive to succeed. I strongly believe in kindness, respect, integrity and transparency, and empowering those around me to be their best and support them to achieve their dreams. I believe that we grow in this world by helping each other.
